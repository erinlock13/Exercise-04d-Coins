Project Name: The ONE Button
MSCH-C220 Final Project

Theme
How you thought about or interpreted the theme
I knew I wanted to go the story route because I thought one button controls seemed too limiting. I immediately thought about losing a button, something I've done a lot, and adding from there.

Game Play
Objective Game-play instructions
Use the arrow keys to move, talk to your grandpa and try to find his button! You might find other buttons, but dont be distracted. There's a total of 5 buttons, but only one matters to Gramps!

What did you
Struggles or surprises you encountered during the development of the game
I don't think I totally absorbed the tilemap lesson, so I was struggling quite a bit there. The art turning out blurry was perhaps the most frustrating part.
